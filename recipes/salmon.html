Silly Salmon Recipe

This is a classic salmon recipe that is sure to leave you and your guests very satisfied and in good graces with the big man upstairs.

You take fresh alaskan salmon and leave it raw. Like actually do not do anything to it. Salt water fish are fit for human consumption raw and it is better to eat food the way god intended. if you have issues with this take it up with God.

Ingredients

  • Salmon (preferably Alaskan)
  • Lemon juice
  • An appreciation for the natural beauty of the fish
  • Love

Cooking instructions

  1. Buy salmon (fresh not frozen)
  2. Gaze upon salmon
  3. Plate salmon on your nicest wooden cutting board
  4. Let salmon get to room temperature
  5. Sprinkle with lemon juice and rosemary then serve
  6. Eat with hand and mouth, tearing off chunks like Gollum
  7. Offer up a small portion to God as a sacrifice (he accepts all sacrifices) then symbolically dispose of in trash

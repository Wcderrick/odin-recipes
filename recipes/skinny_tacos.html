Skinny Tacos

Struggling with your diet goals and generally feeling like a big fat fuck? These delicious and low calorie snack are sure to fix both of the previously mentioned things. Remember to only have 1 per 30KG of body weight!

Ingredients

  • Taco shell
  • Ice
  • Salt
  • lettuce (optional)
  • celery (optional)
  • Lemon Juice

Cooking instructions

  1. Unpackage the taco shells and place them into the oven on 350 for 5 minutes
  2. While the tacos cook pour yourself several large glasses of water and add ice
  3. Drink the waters while the shells heat up, this will help the tacos feel more filling
  4. After 5 minutes, remove the warmed shells from the oven
  5. fill them with your filling of choice, lettuce if you want that crisp texture, celery if you want some added crunch or add nothing if your a purist like i am.
  6. Enjoy
  7. Have several more cups of ice water following the meal to continue that feeling of satiety
  8. Remember, Nothing tastes as good as skinny feels
